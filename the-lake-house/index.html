The Lake House on Cass Lake, LLC

The sophisticated LAKE HOUSE ON CASS LAKE, LLC is tastefully appointed and features leather sofas, ceramic and hardwood floors, natural fireplace, central air, HDTVs, and Wi-Fi.

The oak and granite kitchen is equipped with plenty of china and wine glasses for your friends and family. The dining room offers a beautiful view of the bay. The private courtyard Trex deck is on two levels and can accommodate 20 guests under three umbrella tables with a gas barbecue grill. Near the lake, there are two double "Big Daddy" Adirondack chairs and a bonfire pit.

In the Great Room, the large windows really bring the outdoors in. The Great Room has oak flooring and an 18-foot ceiling with large windows offering a panoramic view of the bay. It is also appointed with a bathroom with shower. Both bathrooms are equipped with elevated toilets. The Lakefront Bedroom has a king-size bed with access to the deck. The Merlot Bedroom has a king-size bed and memory foam rollaway bed. Both the Merlot Bedroom and the Lakefront Bedroom are outfitted with HDTVs.

THE LAKE HOUSE ON CASS LAKE, LLC also has a loft above the living room and is accessible by a ladder up the wall. Children love this space! You cannot stand in the loft. There is a futon mattress for lounging. We do not consider this a bedroom, just a delightful space. There is a two-person limit for the loft.

There are cleats for docking two boats and a swim dock with ladder along the boardwalk. In the evening, gather around the bonfire, tell some tales, and make new memories with your friends and family.

All bathrooms have comfort-level toilets and all bathtubs have grab bars.

We have a strict no pet policy.

THE LAKE HOUSE ON CASS LAKE, LLC sleeps 5.
